biography.

Alexander Sokolov was born on June 24, 1998 in Moscow.

Since childhood, he has been passionate about books and magazines, spending much of his time reading and studying print publications. Later, he began to try his hand at criticism — writing reviews, analyzing texts, searching for his own voice.

In 2020, he graduated from Lomonosov Moscow State University with a degree in Journalism (Educational program "International Journalism").

In 2026, he founded the online journal The Vertical. He is currently working on a book, "The Matter of Silence".

«Criticism is a way of dialogue, and dialogue is a way to understand»
02

journal The Vertical

The Vertical — the axis of meaning
Founded February 8, 2026

The Vertical is an online magazine (media) about people, their inner world, and the stories behind the facade of publicity. We do not chase news or superficial facts. Our focus is thoughts, feelings, principles, personal scenarios, and the search for meaning.

Each piece is an attempt to look beyond the obvious and truly understand a person. We conduct deep, human interviews with no room for hasty conclusions. Only voice, intonation, pauses. Only what constitutes the vertical of a personality.

— behind the facade, there is always someone. we seek the vertical.
